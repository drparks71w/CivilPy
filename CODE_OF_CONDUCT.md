# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

CivilPy is a professional engineering tool. Participants are expected to behave accordingly.

**Expected behavior:**

- Be direct and technically precise. Vague or unsupported claims waste everyone's time.
- Respect the professional liability that engineers carry. Do not pressure others to merge or
  approve work they have not independently verified.
- Give and receive technical criticism plainly. "This calculation has an error at line 47" is
  more useful than hedged language, and more useful than personal criticism.
- Acknowledge when you are outside your area of expertise.

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language of any kind.
- Submitting engineering calculations you have not verified, without disclosing that.
- Misrepresenting your credentials or identity.
- Using this project's issue tracker or merge requests for spam or off-topic promotion.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces: the issue tracker, merge requests, commit messages, and any
communication carried out in the context of this project.

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to [Dane@daneparks.com](mailto:Dane@daneparks.com). Reports will be
handled confidentially. The maintainer reserves the right to remove contributions or block
participants who violate these standards.
